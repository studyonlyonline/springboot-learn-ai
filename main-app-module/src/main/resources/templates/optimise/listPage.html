Frequently Asked Questions about Lead Acid/VRLA batteries

    Lead acid/VRLA electric scooter battery comes with 1 year battery warranty. Battery generally lasts for 1.5 years to 2 years in general.

  2. What is the electric scooty battery starting price ?

    Electric scooty battery price for lead acid battery variant starts from Rs 12,000 for 48V 28AH (Battery set of 4).

  3. What are top brands for e bike lead acid batteries ?

  5. What is the mileage of 12V 28Ah lead acid battery?

    12 volt 28 Ah lead acif battery gives mileage of 50 to 60 km per charge however mileage depends on the driving conditions and Rider's way of driving.

  6. Are lead acid battery for electric scooter good ?

    Yes, lead acid battery for electric scooter are still in high demand because of low cost. They are Still the safest option as they do not catch fire.

  7. Can you convert lead acid battery electric scooter to lithium battery electric scooter ?

    Yes you can convert to lithium battery electric scooter however it requires some modifications in the wiring. It is highly recommended to get it done by an expert electric scooter mechanic.
